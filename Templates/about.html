WE ARE HERE YOU

RESOLVEHUB, developed as a semester project for the CS232 (DBMS) course, aims to address the daily life challenges faced by GIKIANS. By providing a user-friendly platform for lodging complaints, RESOLVEHUB streamlines the complaint resolution process and enhances overall student satisfaction. Through this initiative, GIKI students can contribute to improving the quality of life on campus, making it a more conducive environment for academic pursuits and personal growth.
